Everything to everyone
Everything to Everyone is the debut extended play by American actress and singer Reneé Rapp. It was released on November 11, 2022, through Interscope Records. The extended play was preceded by two singles, "In the Kitchen" and "Don't Tell My Mom", with the third single, "Too Well", released alongside the EP.
Snow angel
Snow Angel is the debut studio album by American singer-songwriter Reneé Rapp. It was released on August 18, 2023, through Interscope. The deluxe version of the album was released on November 17, 2023, featuring a remix of “Tummy Hurts” featuring singer/actress Coco Jones and three new songs.
Snow angel deluxe
Snow Angel (Deluxe) is the special edition of American singer-songwriter Reneé Rapp’s debut album Snow Angel, originally released on August 18, 2023. It was officially announced for release on November 4, 2023. The deluxe version contains four extra tracks with three previously unreleased tracks and a remix of “Tummy Hurts”.
Mean girls
Jeff Richmond and Nell Benjamin returned to the stage musical for the adaptation. Alongside Richmond and Benjamin, Reneé Rapp co-wrote, "What Ifs" replacing "It Roars" from the stage production. Other songs removed from the original score include "Where Do You Belong?", "Fearless", "Stop", "Whose House Is This?", "More Is Better", and "Do This Thing"
